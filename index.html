About Us

Family owned and operated pizza shop. We love to eat pizza. We are so passionate about our pizza and how pizza tastes we decided to open our own pizza shop to share our passion with you. Although our favorite type of pizza to make and enjoy is sausage pizza we fully understand there are many different types of pizza that are also good to others. Everyone is welcome to try our pizza but dont forget we love to serve those who love to have sausage pizza!
